# Sanctuary Identity & Foundation

Faith Frontier is a Christian, non-denominational witness to Jesus Christ. We confess His life, death, and resurrection; we affirm the Trinity; and we walk under Scripture as our guide. Raised in tradition and refined by adversity, we choose devotion over empty labels and name Jesus Christ as the Way, the Truth, and the Life.
